About Square Eyes

We are a team of passionate film-lovers who believe that everyone should have access to high-quality films. Our mission is to connect filmmakers with content watchers and provide a platform for small filmmakers to showcase their work. We believe that everyone has a story to tell and we want to help you discover them.

Discover Great Films

Our website is designed to make it easy for you to find great collections of films and browse through different categories. Whether you’re looking for the latest indie film or a classic masterpiece, we’ve got you covered. Our platform allows filmmakers to sell their films directly to content watchers, which means that you can support small filmmakers and discover interesting films that you won’t find anywhere else.

Support Small Filmmakers at Square Eyes

we’re committed to supporting small filmmake rs. By purchasing films directly from the filmmakers themselves, you’re helping them continue to create amazing content. We believe that everyone deserves a chance to tell their story, and we’re proud to provide a platform for filmmakers to do just that.

Constantly Improving

We’re committed to providing the best possible experience for our users. We’re constantly updating our platform with new features and improvements, so be sure to check back often. We hope you enjoy Square Eyes and discover some amazing films!
